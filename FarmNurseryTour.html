Farm and Nursery Tours   

We are small family run business and are open just about every day. We do not have set hours of operation but you can call us anytime and we should be able to accommodate your visit unless we are away from the farm on errands, deliveries, or installations.

We have had the pleasure of hosting an array of clubs and school groups at our farm.

We provide a 30 to 60 minute educational tour that we cater for older and younger attendees. We can also include a fun arts and crafts build of bamboo pens for the kids to take a great souvenir home with them.
