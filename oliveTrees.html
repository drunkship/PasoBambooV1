Olives Trees 

We purchased our farm in the year 2000 and with that, we acquired 6 acres of recently planted mission variety olive trees. Over the years we have maintained the trees and now we have transplanted some of them out of the ground into 3-foot boxes. These trees have sat in the boxes for over a year and thus have gone through their transplant shock and have established nicely. These olive trees are over 20 years old!
